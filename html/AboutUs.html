Who are we?

BLUE stands for Bringing Life to Underwater Ecosystems. We are a non-profit organization created by passionate ocean advocates to raise awareness about marine life and protect aquatic biodiversity.

Our Mission

We aim to educate the public on the beauty and fragility of underwater ecosystems. Through workshops, educational content, field projects, and citizen science tools, we strive to reconnect people with the ocean.

Our Values

  • Passion for marine conservation
  • Education for all ages
  • Scientific approach & collaboration
  • Inclusion and sustainability

Meet the Team

We are Sophie Jaffal and Aube Ramassamy, marine enthusiasts and co-founders of BLUE. We believe that protecting oceans starts with understanding them. That’s why we created this platform: to share knowledge, fun quizzes, and marine data with everyone!
